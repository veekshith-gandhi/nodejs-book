# ☕ VELORA FILTER COFFEE (Coffee + Chicory Blend)

## 🔥 STEP 1: Sourcing
- Buy **green beans**
  - Arabica (Chikmagalur estate)
  - Robusta (Chikmagalur/Coorg)
- Buy **chicory (roasted root powder)**

👉 Use good quality chicory (affects thickness & taste)

---

## 🔥 STEP 2: Roasting (coffee beans only)

### 👉 Coffee Blend
- Arabica + Robusta (typical: 80/20 or 70/30)
- Roast: **Dark**
- Stop: at **start of second crack**
- Look: dark brown, slight oil

👉 Dark roast gives traditional strong filter taste

---

## ❄️ STEP 3: Cooling
- Immediately spread on tray  
- Use fan, stir → **cool in 5 mins**

---

## ⏳ STEP 4: Resting (Degassing)
- Keep beans **12–24 hours**
- Container slightly open (gas release)

---

## ⚙️ STEP 5: Grinding
- Grind **fine (filter grind)**  
👉 Slightly finer than drip, not as fine as espresso

---

## ⚖️ STEP 6: Mixing with Chicory
- Mix ratio:
  - **80% coffee + 20% chicory** (standard)
  - Can go 70/30 for stronger thickness

- Example:
  - 800g coffee powder
  - 200g chicory

👉 Mix evenly

---

## 📦 STEP 7: Packaging
- Use:
  - **Airtight / valve bags**
  - 250g / 500g packs  

**On pack write:**
- "Filter Coffee (with Chicory)"
- Ratio: 80/20
- Roast level: Dark
- Notes: *Strong, thick, traditional South Indian*

---

## 🛒 STEP 8: Selling
- Website / Instagram / Amazon  
- Highlight:
  - "Authentic South Indian filter coffee"
  - "Rich decoction"
  - "Perfect with milk"

---

# ☕ CUSTOMER USAGE GUIDE

## 🧑‍🍳 South Indian Filter (best method)
1. Add **2–3 tbsp coffee powder** (top chamber)  
2. Press gently  
3. Pour **hot water**  
4. Close lid & wait **10–15 mins**  
5. Collect decoction (bottom)  
6. Mix with **hot milk + sugar**

👉 Taste: thick, strong, aromatic

---

## ⚡ Quick Method (no filter)
1. Add **1–2 tsp powder**  
2. Pour hot water  
3. Let settle → strain if needed  
4. Add milk  

---

## 🎯 Brewing ratio
- **1:10 → 1g coffee = 10ml water** (strong decoction)

---

## 🧠 Storage Instructions
- Store in **airtight container**  
- Use within **2–3 weeks**  
- Keep away from moisture  

---

## 🔥 Final Flow
👉 Buy beans → Roast → Cool → Rest → Grind → Mix chicory → Pack → Sell → Brew
