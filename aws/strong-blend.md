# ☕ VELORA STRONG BLEND (60% Arabica / 40% Robusta)

## 🔥 STEP 1: Sourcing
- Buy **green beans**
  - Arabica (Chikmagalur estate)
  - Robusta (Chikmagalur/Coorg)

👉 Focus on bold varieties (Robusta quality matters more here)

---

## 🔥 STEP 2: Roasting (separately)

### 👉 Arabica (60%)
- Roast: **Medium-dark**
- Stop: after first crack, nearing second crack
- Look: darker brown, slight oil

### 👉 Robusta (40%)
- Roast: **Dark**
- Stop: at start of **second crack**
- Look: dark brown, oily surface

👉 Roast darker to achieve strong, bold taste

---

## ❄️ STEP 3: Cooling
- Immediately spread on tray  
- Use fan, stir → **cool in 5 mins**

---

## ⏳ STEP 4: Resting (Degassing)
- Keep beans **12–24 hours**
- Container slightly open (gas release)

---

## ⚖️ STEP 5: Blending
- Weigh properly:
  - **600g Arabica**
  - **400g Robusta**
- Mix evenly

---

## ⚙️ STEP 6: Grinding (2 options)

### Option A (best):
- Sell **whole beans**

### Option B:
- Grind based on use:
  - Filter → slightly coarse
  - Espresso → fine
  - South Indian filter → fine

---

## 📦 STEP 7: Packaging
- Use:
  - **Valve bags (important)**
  - 250g / 500g packs  

**On pack write:**
- "Strong Blend 60/40"
- Roast level: Medium-dark to Dark
- Notes: *Bold, intense, high caffeine*

---

## 🛒 STEP 8: Selling
- Website / Instagram / Amazon  
- Highlight:
  - "Strongest blend"
  - "High caffeine kick"
  - "Perfect for milk coffee"

---

# ☕ CUSTOMER USAGE GUIDE

## 🧑‍🍳 Option 1: South Indian Filter Coffee (best use)
1. Add **2 tbsp coffee powder**  
2. Pour hot water in filter  
3. Wait 10–15 mins → decoction  
4. Add milk + sugar  

👉 Taste: strong, thick, bold

---

## ☕ Option 2: Espresso
1. Add **fine grind (18g)**  
2. Brew using machine  
3. Extract 25–30 sec  

👉 Strong shot, high caffeine

---

## ⚡ Option 3: Strong Black Coffee
1. Add **1–2 tsp powder**  
2. Pour hot water  
3. Stir → drink  

---

## 🎯 Brewing ratio
- **1:12 → 1g coffee = 12ml water** (stronger than house blend)

---

## 🧠 Storage Instructions
- Store in **airtight container**  
- Use within **2–3 weeks**  
- Grind fresh for best taste

---

## 🔥 Final Flow
👉 Buy beans → Roast → Cool → Rest → Blend → Pack → Sell → Brew
