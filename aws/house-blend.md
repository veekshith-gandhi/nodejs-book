# ☕ VELORA HOUSE BLEND (70% Arabica / 30% Robusta)

## 🔥 STEP 1: Sourcing
- Buy **green beans**
  - Arabica (Chikmagalur estate)
  - Robusta (Chikmagalur/Coorg)

👉 Quality here = final taste

---

## 🔥 STEP 2: Roasting (separately)

### 👉 Arabica (70%)
- Roast: **Medium**
- Stop: just after **first crack**
- Look: brown, dry

### 👉 Robusta (30%)
- Roast: **Medium-dark**
- Stop: slightly before **second crack**
- Look: darker, slight oil

👉 Roast separately for control

---

## ❄️ STEP 3: Cooling
- Immediately spread on tray  
- Use fan, stir → **cool in 5 mins**

---

## ⏳ STEP 4: Resting (Degassing)
- Keep beans **12–24 hours**
- Container slightly open (gas release)

---

## ⚖️ STEP 5: Blending
- Weigh properly:
  - **700g Arabica**
  - **300g Robusta**
- Mix evenly

---

## ⚙️ STEP 6: Grinding (2 options)

### Option A (best):
- Sell **whole beans**

### Option B:
- Grind based on use:
  - Filter → coarse
  - Espresso → fine
  - Drip → medium

---

## 📦 STEP 7: Packaging
- Use:
  - **Valve bags (important)**
  - 250g / 500g packs  

**On pack write:**
- "House Blend 70/30"
- Roast level: Medium
- Notes: *Balanced, smooth, mild strong*

---

## 🛒 STEP 8: Selling
- Website / Instagram / Amazon  
- Highlight:
  - "Chikmagalur origin"
  - "Freshly roasted"
  - "Balanced everyday coffee"

---

# ☕ CUSTOMER USAGE GUIDE

## 🧑‍🍳 Option 1: Filter Coffee (Indian style)
1. Add **2 tbsp coffee powder**  
2. Pour hot water in filter  
3. Wait 10–15 mins → decoction  
4. Add milk + sugar  

👉 Taste: smooth + strong

---

## ☕ Option 2: French Press
1. Add **coarse coffee (15g)**  
2. Pour hot water  
3. Wait 4 mins  
4. Press & serve  

---

## ⚡ Option 3: Quick Brew
1. Add **1–2 tsp powder**  
2. Pour hot water  
3. Stir → drink  

---

## 🎯 Brewing ratio
- **1:15 → 1g coffee = 15ml water**

---

## 🧠 Storage Instructions
- Store in **airtight container**  
- Use within **2–3 weeks**  
- Grind fresh for best taste

---

## 🔥 Final Flow
👉 Buy beans → Roast → Cool → Rest → Blend → Pack → Sell → Brew
